Brown Butter Toffee Cookies

After showing you some very umami and savoruy meals to quench your appetite, it's time to finish these recipes off with a bang. Brown Butter Toffee Cookies, one of my favourite desserts of all time contain the amazing chocolate chip cookie with brown butter and toffee incorporated into it for the best possible twist to an amazing dessert.


Ingredients

For the Toffee

  • 6 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp salt

For the Cookie Dough

  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups dark brown sugar
  • 2 tsp espresso powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 bar dark chocolate

Steps

  1. Start by making the toffee in a non-stick pan, melt the butter, brown sugar and salt and stir together
  2. Once the mixture reaches 290F, pour the mixture onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and let cool in the freezer
  3. Next to make the cookie dough, start by browning the butter
  4. In a pot, add the stick of butter in and heat it up
  5. Once you start to see browned milk solids at the bottom of the pot, kill the heat and add your ice cubes into the butter one at a time
  6. In a large bowl, combine the granulated and brown sugars, espresso powder, salt, and baking soda
  7. Pour the brown butter into the bowl and mix until combined
  8. In another small bowl, combine the eggs and vanilla and then mix that into the large bowl and stir
  9. Remove the toffee from the freezer and smash it in a large freezing bag to break it into small pieces
  10. Chop the chocolate bar and incorporate both the smashed toffee and chopped chocolate in the large bowl
  11. Roll the dough into large dough balls, this recipe should make about 9 large dough balls
  12. When ready to bake line a baking sheet with parchment and preheat the oven to 325 F
  13. Equally distribute the cookies onto the baking sheet and bake for 18-20 minutes in the oven
  14. Once finished, let the cookies rest on the counter to cool and solidfy before enjoying with a cold glass of milk
