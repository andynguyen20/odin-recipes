Avocado Salmon Roll

Avocado Salmon Rolls, one of my favourite creations in history, this sushi roll contains the undefeated duo of raw salmon and creamy avocado paired together to create undeniably the most umami flavours to touch one's tongue.


Ingredients

  • Sushi grade salmon
  • 2 cups uncooked japanese short-grain rice
  • 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 avocado

Steps

  1. Start by washing and cooking your rice
  2. Once the rice is done, start preparing the sushi rice
  3. Add the rice into a large mixing bowl along with the vinegar, oil, sugar, and salr
  4. Mix everything together
  5. Slice your salmon into a small long strip for the roll
  6. Cut your avocado and slice it into thin strips
  7. Lay your rice onto a cutting board wrapped in plastic wrap or a sushi roller if you have very thinly in a rectangle shape
  8. Lay your salmon and avocado strips horizontally across the rice
  9. Using the plastic wrap or sushi roller, roll the ingredients into a sushi roll
  10. Pair with your desired sauce or alone and enjoy
